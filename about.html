Who is Preasx24?

Developer Innovator Student Community Builder

Preasx24 is the pseudonymous founder behind the DTECH ecosystem, a South African tech entrepreneur dedicated to youth empowerment through accessible digital solutions.

His mission centers on empowering youth—especially students and emerging creators in South Africa—through practical technology solutions. He believes in building tools that solve real problems, then breaking them down to make them understandable, sharing them freely, and continuously improving them.

From humble beginnings, Preasx24 has grown DTECH into a multi-platform ecosystem serving thousands of students and developers with free, accessible tools.
