DTECH: Free Tech Empowerment

DTECH is a digital ecosystem built to empower through free technology, education, and innovation. Founded by Preasx24, we provide accessible tools without logins, subscriptions, or payments.

The Founder

Preasx24

Preasx24 is the pseudonymous founder behind DTECH, a South African tech entrepreneur focused on youth empowerment through accessible digital tools. Since 2018, he has built DTECH from a small Facebook page into a multi-platform ecosystem serving thousands.

His approach combines grassroots practicality with technical innovation, creating tools that students and young developers can actually use. From Termux utilities to anime streaming and student resources, every DTECH platform reflects this commitment to open access.
