The Visionary Behind DTECH

Meet Preasx24, the enigmatic founder driving DTECH's mission

Preasx24

Preasx24 is the online alias of South African tech entrepreneur Lefa Mochebane, the visionary founder of DTECH. While little is known about his private life, his commitment to open access and youth empowerment shines through in every project bearing the DTECH name.

Began his journey in early 2018 under the name "Destroyers Tech," initially launching a Facebook page that appears to have been the first public DTECH presence.

Preasx24 drives initiatives like free website sponsorships for student entrepreneurs and small business owners, giving them a digital presence in exchange for small branding credits. His approach blends grassroots practicality with a futuristic mindset, creating a full-circle experience where access, innovation, and growth are central.

From Telegram channels and animated splash screens to GitHub-hosted educational platforms and VPN setups, Preasx24's brand is a hands-on commitment to building the kind of digital ecosystem that young people can actually use, build on, and benefit from.
